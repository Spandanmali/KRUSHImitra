Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of KRUSHImitra? +
Our website provides information and resources for sustainable agriculture practices to empower farmers.
How does climate change affect agriculture development in India? +
Climate change brings unpredictable weather, rising temperatures, and extreme events like droughts and floods, which threaten crop yields and farmers’ livelihoods. Farmers are adopting climate-smart techniques and drought-resistant crops, with ongoing support from the government and scientists to adapt to these changes and safeguard food security.
What is organic farming, and how is it related to agricultural development in India? +
Organic farming avoids synthetic chemicals, focusing on eco-friendly practices that enhance soil health and biodiversity. Growing consumer demand for chemical-free food has spurred organic farming in India, especially in states like Sikkim and Kerala, aligning with sustainable agricultural development goals.
What is agricultural devlopment? +
Agriculture development addresses today’s farming and food production challenges, extending beyond growing crops to include climate change, urbanization, nutrition, and gender equality. Experts focus on building resilient food systems, improving nutrition, and managing natural resources sustainably.
How can young people in India get involved in agricultural development? +
Young people can engage in agriculture through education, agri-entrepreneurship, and technology. Startups in areas like hydroponics and urban agriculture are transforming the sector, and government schemes provide support for new ventures.
